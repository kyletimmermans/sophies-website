About Me


Bio

Sophie Keplinger is a New York City based actor who’s most notable roles in NYC include the title role Lysistrata in the Greek play Lysistrata, Dr. Van Helsing in the play Dracula, and The Soccer Mom in the play The Wolves during her time at The American Academy of Dramatic Arts.


Originally from Nebraska, Sophie moved to New York City at the age of 20 to start her Meisner-based training at The Actors Studio. Upon completing their summer program, she then applied to the 2 year full-time acting school The American Academy of Dramatic Arts, where she earned her Associates Degree in Acting in 2022.
