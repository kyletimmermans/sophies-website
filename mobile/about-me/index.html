About Me

Sophie Keplinger is a Nebraska born actress currently residing in New York City. She studied theatre performance at The American Academy of Dramatic Arts as well as studying the Meisner technique at The Acting Studio in midtown. She has been the lead in over a dozen stage performances as well as a few film projects, in hopes of narrowing her work to more screen acting.
